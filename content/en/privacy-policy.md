### Tracking Technologies and Cookies
We use Cookies and similar tracking tech to keep tabs on activity on our Service and stash away certain info. The tracking tech we use includes beacons, tags, and scripts, all for collecting and tracking info, and to tweak and analyze our Service. The tech we might use includes:

-   **Cookies or Browser Cookies**: A Cookie is a small file chilling on Your Device. You can tell your browser to say "no thanks" to all Cookies or to give you a heads-up when one's being sent. But hey, if you don't take Cookies, you might not be able to use some parts of our Service. Unless you've fiddled with your browser settings to reject Cookies, our Service might just go ahead and use them.
-   **Web Beacons**: Certain sections of our Service and our emails might have tiny electronic files called web beacons (also known as clear gifs, pixel tags, and single-pixel gifs). These let the Company, for example, count users who've visited those pages or opened an email, and for other related website stats (like noting how popular a certain section is and checking the integrity of the system and server).

Cookies can be "Persistent" or "Session" Cookies. Persistent Cookies stick around on your personal computer or mobile device even when you go offline, while Session Cookies get the boot the moment you close your web browser.

We use both Session and Persistent Cookies for the purposes laid out below:

-   **Necessary / Essential Cookies**
    -   **Type**: Session Cookies
    -   **Administered by**: Us
    -   **Purpose**: These Cookies are a must-have to give you the services available through the Website and to let you use some of its cool features. They help verify users and stop dodgy use of user accounts. Without these Cookies, the services you've asked for can't be delivered, and we only use these Cookies to hook you up with those services.

-   **Cookies Policy / Notice Acceptance Cookies**
    -   **Type**: Persistent Cookies
    -   **Administered by**: Us
    -   **Purpose**: These Cookies figure out if users have given the green light to the use of Cookies on the Website.

-   **Functionality Cookies**
    -   **Type**: Persistent Cookies
    -   **Administered by**: Us
    -   **Purpose**: These Cookies let us remember the choices you make when you're using the Website, like recalling your login deets or language preferences. The whole point of these Cookies is to give you a more personal experience and save you the hassle of having to punch in your preferences every single time you hit up the Website.

For more details about the Cookies we use and your choices regarding Cookies, hit up our Cookies Policy or the Cookies section of our Privacy Policy.
