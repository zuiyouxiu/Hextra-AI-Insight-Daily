### Tracking Technologies and Cookies
We use Cookies and similar tracking tech to keep tabs on activity on our Service and store certain info. The tracking tech we use includes beacons, tags, and scripts to gather and track info and to boost and analyze our Service. The tech we use might include:

- **Cookies or Browser Cookies**: A Cookie is a tiny file placed on your device. You can tell your browser to refuse all Cookies or to give you a heads-up when a Cookie is being sent. However, if you don't play along with Cookies, you might not be able to use certain parts of our Service. Unless you've tweaked your browser settings to say no to Cookies, our Service might just go ahead and use them.
- **Web Beacons**: Certain sections of our Service and our emails might contain small electronic files known as web beacons (also called clear GIFs, pixel tags, and single-pixel GIFs). These files let the Company, for instance, count users who've visited those pages or opened an email, and for other related website stats (like checking out how popular a certain section is and making sure the system and server are running smoothly).

Cookies can be "Persistent" or "Session" Cookies. Persistent Cookies stick around on your personal computer or mobile device even when you go offline, while Session Cookies get wiped the moment you close your web browser.

We use both Session and Persistent Cookies for the purposes listed below:

- **Necessary / Essential Cookies**
  - **Type**: Session Cookies
  - **Managed by**: Us
  - **Purpose**: These Cookies are super important for dishing out the services available through the Website and letting you use some of its cool features. They help authenticate users and prevent dodgy use of user accounts. Without these Cookies, the services you've asked for can't really happen, and we only use these Cookies to get those services to you.

- **Cookies Policy / Notice Acceptance Cookies**
  - **Type**: Persistent Cookies
  - **Managed by**: Us
  - **Purpose**: These Cookies figure out if users have given the green light to using Cookies on the Website.

- **Functionality Cookies**
  - **Type**: Persistent Cookies
  - **Managed by**: Us
  - **Purpose**: These Cookies let us remember the choices you make when you use the Website, like recalling your login details or language preferences. The goal of these Cookies is to give you a more personal experience and save you the hassle of re-entering your preferences every time you use the Website.

For more info about the Cookies we use and your options regarding them, take a peek at our Cookies Policy or the Cookies section of our Privacy Policy.
