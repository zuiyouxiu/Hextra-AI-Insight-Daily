### Tracking Technologies and Cookies
We use Cookies and similar tracking tech to keep tabs on activity on our Service and store certain info. The tracking tech we use includes beacons, tags, and scripts to gather and track info, and to boost and analyze our Service. The tech we use might include:

- **Cookies or Browser Cookies**: A Cookie is a tiny file placed on your Device. You can tell your browser to say "no thanks" to all Cookies or to give you a heads-up when one is being sent. But hey, if you don't roll with Cookies, you might not be able to use some parts of our Service. Unless you've set your browser to reject Cookies, our Service might just use them.
- **Web Beacons**: Certain sections of our Service and our emails might have tiny electronic files called Web Beacons (also known as clear gifs, pixel tags, and single-pixel gifs). These little guys let the Company, for example, count users who have dropped by those pages or opened an email, and for other related website stats (like checking how popular a certain section is and making sure the system and server are running smoothly).

Cookies can be "Persistent" or "Session" Cookies. Persistent Cookies stick around on your personal computer or mobile device when you go offline, while Session Cookies vanish the moment you close your web browser.

We use both Session and Persistent Cookies for these purposes:

- **Necessary / Essential Cookies**
  - **Type**: Session Cookies
  - **Administered by**: Us
  - **Purpose**: These Cookies are an absolute must for getting you the services available through the Website and letting you use some of its cool features. They help authenticate users and prevent sneaky, fraudulent use of user accounts. Without these Cookies, the services you're asking for can't be delivered, and we only use these Cookies to give you those services.

- **Cookies Policy / Notice Acceptance Cookies**
  - **Type**: Persistent Cookies
  - **Administered by**: Us
  - **Purpose**: These Cookies are for figuring out if users have given the green light to the use of Cookies on the Website.

- **Functionality Cookies**
  - **Type**: Persistent Cookies
  - **Administered by**: Us
  - **Purpose**: These Cookies let us remember the choices you make when you use the Website, like remembering your login deets or language preferences. The whole point of these Cookies is to give you a more personalized experience and save you the hassle of having to re-enter your preferences every single time you use the Website.

For more info about the Cookies we use and your options regarding Cookies, hit up our Cookies Policy or the Cookies section of our Privacy Policy.
