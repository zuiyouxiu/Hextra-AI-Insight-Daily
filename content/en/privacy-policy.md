We use these bad boys to collect and track info, and to seriously level up and analyze our Service. The tech we might use includes:

Unless you've tweaked your browser settings to reject Cookies, our Service might just use them anyway.
- **Web Beacons**: Some parts of our Service and our emails might have these tiny electronic files called Web Beacons (also known as clear GIFs, pixel tags, and single-pixel GIFs). Cookies can be either "Persistent" or "Session" Cookies. Persistent Cookies chill on your personal computer or mobile device even when you go offline, while Session Cookies vanish into thin air as soon as you close your web browser.

We use both Session and Persistent Cookies for the following reasons:

- **Cookies Policy / Acceptance Notice Cookies**
  - **Type**: Persistent Cookies
  - **Administered by**: Us
  - **Purpose**: These Cookies are used to check if users have given their thumbs up to the use of cookies on the Website.

- **Functionality Cookies**
  - **Type**: Persistent Cookies
