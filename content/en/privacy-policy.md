### Tracking Technologies & Cookies
Cookies and similar tracking technologies? We totally use 'em to keep tabs on activity within our Service and store some info. These tracking technologies include awesome stuff like beacons, tags, and scripts. They're all about collecting and tracking info to help us level up and analyze our Service. The tech we use might include:

Cookies can be "Persistent" or "Session" Cookies. Persistent Cookies stick around on your personal computer or mobile device even when you go offline, while Session Cookies vanish the moment you close your web browser.

We use both Session and Persistent Cookies for the following cool purposes:

- **Necessary / Essential Cookies**
  - **Type**: Session Cookies
  - **Administered by**: Us
  - **Purpose**: These Cookies are absolutely vital to hook you up with services available through the Website and to let you use some of its rad features. They help authenticate users and prevent fraudulent use of user accounts. Without these Cookies, the services you've asked for can't be provided, and we only use these Cookies to give you those services.

- **Cookies Policy / Notice Acceptance Cookies**
  - **Type**: Persistent Cookies
  - **Administered by**: Us
  - **Purpose**: These Cookies are used to figure out if users have given a thumbs-up to the use of cookies on the Website.

- **Functionality Cookies**
  - **Type**: Persistent Cookies
  - **Administered by**: Us
  - **Purpose**: These Cookies empower us to remember the choices you make when you use the Website, like recalling your login deets or language preference. The whole point of these Cookies is to give you a more personalized experience and save you from having to re-enter your preferences every single time you hit up the Website.
